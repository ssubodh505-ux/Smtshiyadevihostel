Welcome to Smt Shiya Devi Hostel

Located just minutes away from DJ Polytechnic College, Baraut, Baghpat, Smt Shiya Devi Hostel has been a trusted home for hundreds of students. We believe every student deserves a safe, clean, and vibrant environment to focus on their studies and future.

From fully equipped rooms to an in-hostel shop, we take care of every need so you can focus on what matters — your education and career.

What We Offer

Facilities

Everything a student needs, all under one roof.

24×7 Electricity

Uninterrupted power supply round the clock. Never worry about load shedding or power cuts again.

Solar Power Backup

Eco-friendly solar energy system ensures green power and zero interruptions even during outages.

RO Pure Water

Advanced Reverse Osmosis (ARO) purification provides 100% safe drinking water 24/7.

Best Rooms

Spacious, well-ventilated rooms with bed, mattress, study table, chair, and storage almirah.

In-Hostel Shop

On-campus store stocked with stationery, snacks, daily essentials, and personal care products.

24×7 Security

Round-the-clock guards, CCTV cameras, and biometric entry for maximum safety of every student.

Water Cooler (Summer)

Chilled cold drinking water available all day during summers. Beat the heat with our in-hostel water cooler facility.

In-Hostel Temple

A peaceful temple right inside the hostel premises. Students can pray and find spiritual calm without stepping outside.
